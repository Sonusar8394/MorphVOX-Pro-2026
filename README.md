## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Does this work with Discord?**
Yes. Open Discord settings, go to Voice & Video, and pick "Screaming Bee Audio" as your input device.

**Can I restore my original voice?**
Yes. Click the "Default" profile in the main window to disable all changes.

**Why does my audio sound distorted?**
Lower the "Timbre" adjustment slider. Excessive changes to your natural voice profile often cause distortion.

**Does it consume much power?**
The software stays lightweight. It uses minimal CPU and RAM while running in the background.

**Will this interfere with my speakers?**
Keep your speakers at a reasonable volume to avoid feedback loops. Using a headset prevents the microphone from picking up sound from your speakers.
